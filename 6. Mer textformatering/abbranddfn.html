Breadcrumbs is a website navigational aid, usually near the top of a page, showing the path you have followed to reach a given page, or where the page you are viewing resides in a website.

Search Engine is a mechanism for finding content on the Internet or on a website. The most familiar Internet search engine is google.com.

HTTP (Hyper Text Transfer Protocol) is the standard for managing communications between web browsers and web servers.

IP address (Internet Protocol Address) is a unique numerical address assigned to every device and resource on the Internet and similar networks.
